Quick Answers

Common Questions

How fast can you respond?

For emergencies, we guarantee arrival within 60 minutes. For standard service, we offer same-day appointments when you call before noon.

Do you charge for estimates?

Written estimates are completely free. There is an $89 service call fee, which is fully credited toward your repair if you proceed with us.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. RapidFlow holds State Master Plumber License #PL-48201 and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance.

What warranty do you offer?

All labor is covered by a 2-year warranty. Parts carry the manufacturer's warranty (typically 1–10 years). We stand behind every job we do.
