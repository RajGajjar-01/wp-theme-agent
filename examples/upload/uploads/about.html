Our Story

From a Single Van to Metro's Best

RapidFlow started in 1999 when founder Marcus Reid traded in a desk job to do what he'd always loved — fixing things. Starting with a single van and a handshake reputation, Marcus built his business the old-fashioned way: show up on time, charge fairly, and never leave a job unfinished.

Today, RapidFlow has grown to a team of 18 certified technicians operating across the Metro Area and surrounding counties. But the core values Marcus started with haven't changed one bit: honesty, quality, and treating every customer like a neighbor — because most of them are.

We're proud to be the plumber friends recommend to friends, generation after generation.
